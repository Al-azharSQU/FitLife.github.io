5-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Bench Press - 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Barbell Rows - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Overhead Press - 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Squats - 4 sets x 15 reps
  • Deadlifts - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets x 12 reps (each leg)

Day 3: Cardio

Choose your favorite cardio activity and perform it for at least 30 minutes.

Day 4: Core

  • Plank - 4 sets x 30 seconds
  • Leg Raises - 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Russian Twists - 3 sets x 20 reps (each side)

Day 5: Active Recovery

Engage in light activities like walking or yoga for recovery.
