Your Premium Workout Plan

Here's your exclusive premium workout plan with the push-pull-legs (PPL) split.

Workout Days

DAY ONE(Push Day)

  1. Bench Press: 3×6-8
  2. Shoulder Press: 3×8-10
  3. Incline Dumbbell Flyes: 3×10-15
  4. Triceps Pushdowns: 3×10-15
  5. Abs: 10 minutes of whatever you want.

DAY TWO(Pull Day)

  1. Rows: 3×6-8
  2. Pull-Ups or Lat Pull-Downs: 3×8-10
  3. Face Pulls: 3×10-15
  4. Barbell Shrugs: 3×8-10
  5. Dumbbell Curls: 3×10-15

DAY THREE(Legs Day)

  1. Squats: 3×6-8
  2. Romanian Deadlifts: 3×6-8
  3. Leg Press: 3×8-10
  4. Leg Curls: 3×8-10
  5. Standing Calf Raises: 3×6-8
  6. Seated Calf Raises: 2×10-15

Remember to perform each workout with proper form and gradually increase intensity over time. Stay consistent and enjoy your fitness journey!
